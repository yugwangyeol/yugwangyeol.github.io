About

I am an incoming graduate student at Korea University, with a B.S. in AI, Big Data & Management from Kookmin University. My research interests span computer vision, generative modeling, and anomaly detection.

I work on generative models such as DDPMs, Stable Diffusion, and 3D generation; visual understanding tasks including object detection, tracking, OCR, segmentation, and explainable AI; and anomaly detection across time-series, video, and image domains. I am also interested in speech applications such as voice conversion and voice defense.
